About Kerala

Kerala is located on the south-western tip of India. The land of Keralaputras or Keram (coconut trees), straddled by the evergreen Western Ghats on one side and the fathomless Arabian Sea on the other, is as different from the rest of India as pepper is to chill. This is this favourable geography which makes Kerala unique.
India enjoys this unique geographical feature that has made it one of the most sought-after tourist destinations. Fondly referred to as 'God's Own Country'.
